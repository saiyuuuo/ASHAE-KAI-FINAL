Biography

ASHAE KAI, the artistic name of Saiyuk Silva Truevas, is a music producer from Peru, born in 2005. His work stands out for blending contemporary electronic sounds with deep cultural and aesthetic influences from Japan, shaping a unique and cinematic sonic identity.

With a strong artistic vision and refined sound design, ASHAE KAI fuses genres like bass, experimental trap, and future beats. His music often features vocal chops, emotive atmospheres, and unconventional structures that immerse listeners in introspective and emotional journeys.

Inspired by artists such as Skrillex, yet maintaining a distinctly personal style, ASHAE KAI creates tracks that go beyond trends—each piece is a carefully crafted world that combines emotion, culture, and innovation. His music aims to connect visuals and sound, offering a multi-sensory experience that breaks boundaries and speaks to a global audience.

Emerging from Peru with an international mindset, ASHAE KAI represents a new generation of independent producers redefining the future of electronic music.
